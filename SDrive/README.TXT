This folder is for storage of projects and resources while in your current course(s). All files and subdirectories will be deleted when you are no longer enrolled at Coleman University.

Please be prudent with the files you store in this folder as you do not have unlimited space.

CIS students are allowed 250 MB of storage.

Faculty have Read-Only access to this folder for grading or instructional purposes. Please double-click on My Computer (if Using Windows XP) or Computer (if using Windows 7) to see other shares that are available to you as a resource for your course.
